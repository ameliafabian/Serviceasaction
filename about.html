About Us

This short and informative website was created by highschool student at Centennial highschool, Amelia Fabian, in hopes to inform people the potential dangers of everyday usage of household chemicals, and prevent any possible injuries/ accidents that come with it. The goal of creating this website was to bring global awareness to the issue of processed chemicals that do not come with many warnings.
