Bleach + Isopropyl alcohol → Chloroform gas

Combining Bleach and Rubbing Alcohol creates a toxic chloroform gas. Chloroform is an organic compound that is typically used as an anesthetic. If you unintentionally create this gas while mixing bleach and alcohol, it could potentially cause irritation to the eyes and respiratory system, skin rashes, damage to nervous system, unconsciousness, or even cancer.
