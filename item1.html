Bleach + Ammonia → Chloramine

Mixing Bleach and Ammonia can create chloramine gas, which is highly toxic for humans to inhale. This mixture can cause a deadly chemical reaction, releasing toxic fumes that can cause sore throats, unconsciousness, and death. Chlorine bleach, which contains sodium hypochlorite, releases chloramine gas into the air, leading to chemical pneumonitis. This can cause irritation to the throat, nose, and eyes, lasting up to 24 hours in mild cases.
