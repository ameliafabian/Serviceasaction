The Dangers of Mixing Household Chemicals

Amelia Fabian

March 19, 2024

Many homeowners may be unsuspecting of possible dangerous chemicals that they use to clean every day and the health implications they may bring. Bringing attention to the issue of mixing chemicals which could produce a toxic or deadly compound could help prevent undesirable consequences. Here are a few household cleaning chemicals that you should avoid mixing.

Here is an interactive list of dangerous chemical compounds. Click on one to learn more about the compound and its consequences.

  • NaClO + NH₃ → NH₂Cl
  • NaClO + CH₃COOH → Cl₂
  • CH₃COOH + H₂O₂ → CH₃CO₃H
  • NaClO + C₃H₈O → CHCl₃
