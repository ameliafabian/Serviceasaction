Vinegar + Peroxide → Peracetic acid

Vinegar and Peroxide mixture creates peracetic acid, which may be highly toxic and could irritate your throat and skin. You can spray these chemicals separately; however, it is crucial to properly wipe each chemical before adding the other, or else you could end up with peracetic acid.
