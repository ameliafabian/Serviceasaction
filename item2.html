Bleach + Vinegar → Chlorine gas

The combination of Bleach and Vinegar is dangerous as it creates chlorine gas, a deadly chemical. While some believe it can create a better disinfectant, it's not safe to use in a household. The American Association of Poison Control Centers reports that over 6,000 incidents of chlorine gas exposure in the U.S. in 2016 were caused by people mixing household chemicals like bleach and vinegar.
